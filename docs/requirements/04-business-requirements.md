# BizHub Platform
# Business Requirements Specification

**Document ID:** BIZHUB-BRS-001  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Status:** Approved  
**Standard:** IEEE 29148

---

# 1. Purpose

This document defines the business requirements that the BizHub platform shall satisfy.

---

# 2. Business Objectives

The platform shall:

- Digitize business administration services
- Reduce manual processing
- Improve client communication
- Provide secure document management
- Automate repetitive workflows
- Increase operational efficiency
- Improve reporting and analytics
- Support future SaaS expansion

---

# 3. Business Drivers

- Increased demand for online business services
- Regulatory compliance
- Improved customer experience
- Reduced operational costs
- Scalable service delivery

---

# 4. Business Capabilities

The platform shall support:

- Company Registration
- Company Amendments
- Director Management
- Compliance Management
- Annual Returns
- Tax Services
- Accounting Services
- Document Management
- Client Portal
- Internal Workflow Management
- Reporting

---

# 5. Business Constraints

- WordPress platform
- WooCommerce integration
- POPIA compliance
- HTTPS-only production
- Modular architecture
- GitHub source control

---

# 6. Business Success Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|---------|--------|
| Client Registration Completion | ≥ 95% |
| Successful Service Orders | ≥ 99% |
| System Availability | ≥ 99.9% |
| Average Application Processing Time | < 5 Business Days |
| Customer Satisfaction | ≥ 90% |
| Support Response Time | < 1 Business Day |

---

# 7. Stakeholders

| Stakeholder | Responsibility |
|-------------|----------------|
| Clients | Purchase and consume services |
| Staff | Process applications |
| Managers | Operational oversight |
| Administrators | Platform administration |
| Business Owners | Strategic direction |
