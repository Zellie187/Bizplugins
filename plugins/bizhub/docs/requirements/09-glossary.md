# BizHub Platform
# Project Glossary

**Document ID:** BIZHUB-SRS-009  
**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Status:** Approved

---

# A

## Application

A client request submitted for a specific business service.

---

## Audit Log

A permanent record of system and user activity.

---

# C

## Client

A registered customer using BizHub services.

---

## Company

A registered legal entity managed within the platform.

---

## Compliance

Activities required to maintain legal standing of a company.

---

# D

## Dashboard

The primary user interface displaying operational information.

---

## Document

Any uploaded or generated electronic file associated with an application.

---

# M

## Module

An independently deployable software component providing business functionality.

---

# N

## Notification

A message delivered to users through email, portal, SMS, or other supported channels.

---

# P

## Portal

The secure client-facing area of the BizHub platform.

---

# R

## Repository

A software component responsible for data access.

---

## Role

A collection of permissions assigned to a user.

---

# S

## Service

A commercial offering provided through the BizHub platform.

---

## Staff

An internal user responsible for processing applications.

---

# U

## User

Any authenticated person interacting with the platform.

---

# W

## Workflow

A defined sequence of business process states governing an application.
