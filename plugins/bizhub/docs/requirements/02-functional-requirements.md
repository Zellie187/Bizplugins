# BizHub Platform
# Functional Requirements

**Document ID:** BIZHUB-SRS-002  
**Version:** 1.0.0

---

# 1. User Management

## FR-001

The system shall allow users to register accounts.

---

## FR-002

The system shall authenticate registered users.

---

## FR-003

The system shall support role-based authorization.

---

## FR-004

The system shall allow password reset using WordPress authentication.

---

# 2. Client Portal

## FR-005

The system shall display a client dashboard.

---

## FR-006

The client shall view purchased services.

---

## FR-007

The client shall upload supporting documentation.

---

## FR-008

The client shall download completed documentation.

---

## FR-009

The client shall monitor workflow progress.

---

# 3. Company Management

## FR-010

The platform shall maintain company profiles.

---

## FR-011

---

## FR-012
